Tracking SA's Stockouts

Since 2014, we've worked with a network of citizen journalists to monitor if certain medicines are in stock at 48 clinics around the country. The medicines we chose followed the Department of Health's own directives, which stipulate mandatory minimum drug availability at recognized healthcare centers.
