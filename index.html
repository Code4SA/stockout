Tracking SA's Stockouts

OurHealth citizen journalists monitor the stock of 22 essential medicines at two clinics per month across the country. The National Department of Health assisted us to select which medicines to monitor and the list covers a wide spectrum from TB medicine to antiretroviral medicine, antibiotics, chronic medication and painkillers. Stock-outs are reported to the NDoH and Stop the Stock-outs.
